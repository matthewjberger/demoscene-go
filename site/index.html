rendergraph-go

Data-oriented Go game engine: archetype ECS, a wgpu-backed render graph with declarative passes, dual-world simulation and renderer separation. Source on GitHub.

  • Engine demo
    A grid of spinning primitives over a procedural sky and ground grid, viewed through a pan-orbit camera, lit by a directional sun.
  • breakout
    3D-mesh breakout clone with a tilted top-down camera. A/D moves the paddle, space launches, R restarts.

Requires a WebGPU-capable browser (recent Chrome, Edge, or Safari 26+).
